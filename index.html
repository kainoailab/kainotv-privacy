KainoTV – Privacy Policy

Last updated:

About KainoTV

KainoTV is a media player for user-provided M3U/M3U8 playlists. The app does not include content, channels, or playlists, and does not unlock or provide access to any third-party services.

Data Collection

KainoTV does not collect, track, store, or share personal data. No analytics or advertising SDKs are used.

User Responsibility

The app only plays streams the user is authorized to access. Users are solely responsible for the playlists they import and for complying with applicable laws, licenses, and rights.

Permissions

  • Network access: required to play user-provided streams.
  • Local files (optional): used to import M3U/M3U8 playlists from the device.
  • AirPlay / Picture in Picture (if available): system features only; no personal data is collected.

Contact
